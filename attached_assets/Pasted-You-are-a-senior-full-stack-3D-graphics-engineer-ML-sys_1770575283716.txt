5️⃣ POSE EDITING (CRITICAL)
Key Constraint

Meshes generated by HybrIK/PIFuHD are static meshes without bones.

Solution

Use auto-rigging pipeline:

Either:

SMPL/SMPL-X parametric skeleton (preferred)

Or external auto-rigging service (e.g. Mixamo-style logic)

Store skeleton + skin weights

Convert to SkinnedMesh

Pose editing is done by rotating bones

Provide:

Preset poses

Sliders for arms/legs/head

Pose editing happens:

Frontend UI

Bone transforms applied in Three.js

Backend stores pose parameters
